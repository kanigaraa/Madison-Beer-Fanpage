Madison Beer

About

Madison Elle Beer is an American singer and songwriter who first gained attention as a teenager after her singing videos went viral online. Her distinctive voice and pop-R&B style quickly captured public interest.


She has released several notable works such as As She Pleases and Life Support, showcasing emotional depth and maturity in her music. Her lyrics often reflect personal experiences and honest feelings.


Achievements


  • Silence Between Songs was nominated for Best Immersive Audio Album at the 2025 Grammy Awards.
  • Make You Mine received a nomination for Best Dance Pop Recording at the 2025 Grammy Awards.
